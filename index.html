Our Story

We're the neighborhood spot on Clark St.

Sweet&Salt started with a simple idea: a cozy place where everyone's welcome — whether you're grabbing a quick espresso, settling in for shakshuka with friends, or sneaking a second slice of tiramisu (we won't judge).

We bake everything in-house with recipes inspired by European traditions and Lincoln Park love. No shortcuts, no compromises — just real ingredients and honest food made for people who appreciate the little things.
